The Warning: Norms vs. Laws

Meacham emphasizes that "Norms" are doing the right thing when the law doesn't force you to. The crisis occurs when leaders abandon norms and only respect the literal letter of the law to seize power.

Glossary

Semiquincentennial: The 250th anniversary.

Norms: Unwritten rules of conduct. Doing the right thing when the law doesn't force you to.

"Better Angels": Lincoln's phrase for humanity's capacity for reason and restraint over fear.

"Interpreted as it ought to be": Douglass's qualifier: reading the Constitution by its principles, not the framers' compromises.
